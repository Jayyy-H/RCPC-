# Attribution types
Choose exactly one type per criterion:

- `supporting_span`: the answer contains a concrete span that substantively supports the criterion.
- `explicit_error`: the answer contains a concrete span showing an error directly related to the criterion.
- `missing`: the required capability is absent and there is no precise local error span.
- `global`: the criterion depends on whole-answer coherence or conclusion consistency and cannot be reliably localized.

# Quote rules
- For `supporting_span` and `explicit_error`, `quote` must be copied exactly from the corresponding Student Answer.
- Choose the smallest span that still explains the attribution.
- Do not rewrite, summarize, correct, or invent text.
- For `missing` and `global`, `quote` must be an empty string.
- Do not quote the Problem, Rubrics, Known Final Answer, reference responses, or other student answers.
